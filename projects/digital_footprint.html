A research-driven mobile app helping users visualize and manage online exposure across social media platforms.

Project Overview

Many social media users unknowingly share personal data despite available privacy controls, putting them at emotional and psychological risk. This project aimed to understand what personal information users expose on Instagram, explore their awareness and emotional responses, and design an app to help them visualize and manage their digital footprint.

Key Objectives

  • Understand user digital exposure patterns across Instagram
  • Provide actionable insights for better privacy management
  • Design an intuitive mobile interface to visualize and control data

Research & Insights

To uncover how users perceive their online exposure, I conducted qualitative and quantitative research combining interviews, surveys, and thematic analysis.

Research Methods

  • Collected and analyzed publicly accessible Instagram data
  • Semi-structured interviews to gather emotional reactions and privacy behaviors
  • Reflexive thematic analysis to extract key insights

Key Insights

  • Users underestimated the scale of their digital exposure (location, health, relationship status, politics)
  • Emotional reactions included surprise, discomfort, anxiety
  • Coping strategies: self-censorship, selective sharing, archiving old posts
  • Users desired clear, visual tools to understand public data

Quantitative Reinforcement

External datasets were used to validate qualitative patterns and strengthen evidence for design decisions.

Interface Screens

The app includes key flows from login to risk reporting, designed for clarity and user empowerment.

Login Screen

Secure and minimalist interface for signing in or registering.

Connect Accounts

Select which social media platforms to analyze for digital footprint insights.

Appeared Risks

Shows risks detected in user data with visual highlights for clarity.

Preview of Shared Information

Allows review and editing of posts containing personal information.

Post Scanner

Simulates a post and gives real-time privacy risk alerts.

Post Simulation

Preview how posts would appear with highlighted potential privacy risks.

Privacy Risk Overview

Summarizes detected threats, including location, personal info, and visibility risks.

Risk Hints

Provides actionable suggestions to reduce exposure.

Report Screen

Generates a downloadable, personalized privacy report tracking changes over time.

Settings

Control connected accounts, app preferences, and privacy checkup history.

Shared Location Data

Displays posts where location was exposed, organized by platform.

Outcome & Impact

  • Raised user awareness of real privacy risks through visual design
  • Enabled users to control and manage online exposure
  • Prototype serves as foundation for future privacy education tools
