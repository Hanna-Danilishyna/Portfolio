Deutsche Bahn Passenger Experience

Contextual research and service design to improve passenger journeys, addressing real pain points and introducing actionable features.

Role: UX Researcher & Designer
Team: 5
Duration: 6 weeks

Problem

Passengers were frustrated by train cancellations, ticketing issues, lack of scenic route notifications, and poor real-time guidance during journeys.

Process & Research Methods

We used a combination of contextual inquiries, affinity mapping, identity mapping, and co-creation workshops.

Affinity Map
Identity Mapping
Wall Walk Session

UX & Interface Design

Based on research insights, we designed new features to improve user experience, including reminders, scenic route notifications, and integration of student passes.

Reminder Feature

Provides a sound notification when the passenger is approaching their destination.

Scenic Route Notification

Alerts passengers of interesting scenic views during their journey.

Full App Flow

Comprehensive view of adding a student pass and accessing main features in the app.

Outcome

  • Introduced actionable reminders and scenic route notifications.
  • Improved passenger guidance and journey satisfaction.
  • Demonstrated value of contextual inquiry and participatory workshops.
