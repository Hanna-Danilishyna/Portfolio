About

I’m a data-focused UX researcher and designer with industry experience, specializing in transforming complex user data into meaningful, ethical digital experiences.

I design research-based systems that help people understand and manage complex information — from privacy risks to behavioral data. By combining qualitative research methods with quantitative analysis tools such as Python, I bridge the gap between human insight and measurable evidence.

My work focuses on transparency, clarity, and empowering users to make informed decisions in digital environments.
